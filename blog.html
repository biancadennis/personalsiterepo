#TechLife

Starting up with the NYCDA

This week I started down the path toward tech. Technically I already work in the industry but I work in data analytics and don't really do much of what you think about when you think tech. No web building. 90's style hacking a la Jurassic Park. No coding.

But this week I took a sharper left on the road toward coding and started class at the New York Code + Design Academy. In the coming weeks I hope to document my experience taking the class part time web development course, figuring out how to apply what I learn to a career, all while preparing to move cross country and hopefully settle in Seattle. My #TechLife series will document some of the ins and outs of the process and maybe help me figure out some things along the way.

Stay tuned in the coming weeks and wish me luck!
